Ingredients:
 - 1 can tuna
 - 2 spoons sweet relish
 - 2 spoons mayonnaise
 - 1/4 chopped red apple
 - 1 slice chedder cheese
 - 2 slices sourdough bread
 - 2 spoons butter
 - 1/2 spoon pepper
 - 1/2 spoon salt

Directions:
 1. Mix tuna, relish, mayonnaise, apple, salt, and pepper.
 2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
 3. Make a cheese sandwich with butter sides of bread on outside.
 4. Grill the cheese sandwich.
 5. Open grilled cheese sandwich and add tuna mix.
